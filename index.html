Playing -Modes

SURVIVE -SOLO

Solos. The classic game mode. Complete free for all, kill everyone, be the last one alive. Solo, a game mode where you spawn into the world alone and you just rely on your own tactics and skill to push you to the end and be the last player alive.

PLAY- DUO TOURNAMENT

For this game mode, you will be paired up with another individual and will compete to be the last ones alive.Duo FPP is based around your normal Duo game mode (as read above), but instead of being in 3rd person this is strictly in 1st person

PLAY-WITH SQUAD

Squad is a gameplay type where players are organized into teams which are then pitted against each other. This is distinct from the classic Free For All (FFA) gameplay mode, where players are free to kill whomever they wish
